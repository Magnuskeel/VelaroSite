Help & FAQ

Common questions about installing and using Velaro

You can install Velaro by downloading it from Microsoft AppSource or by importing the .app file into your Business Central environment.

  1. In Business Central's Extension Management, choose Upload Extension if installing manually
  2. Follow the prompts to upload the Velaro .app file
  3. Once installed, assign the appropriate user permissions
  4. Navigate to the Velaro setup page to begin configuration

After installing Velaro, follow these steps to configure your customer and vendor portal:

  1. Open the Velaro extension setup page in Business Central
  2. Configure portal settings and branding options
  3. Set up user roles and permissions for portal access
  4. Define what information and services customers/vendors can access
  5. Test the portal configuration with a sample user account

Velaro provides flexible access control to manage who can access your portal and what they can see:

  1. In the Velaro user management section, create portal user accounts
  2. Assign appropriate roles (Customer, Vendor, or custom roles)
  3. Configure permissions for each role to control access to specific information
  4. Set up authentication methods for secure portal access
  5. Monitor user activity and adjust permissions as needed
  6. Provide users with their portal login credentials and access instructions

If you need additional help with Velaro, here are your support options:

  • Check the built-in help documentation within the Velaro extension
  • Review the setup guide and troubleshooting tips in your admin portal
  • Contact our support team with your configuration details for personalized assistance
  • Visit our knowledge base for additional resources and updates

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Velaro collects minimal data necessary to provide our portal services. This includes:

  • Contact information (names, business emails of administrators)
  • Portal user details (usernames, roles, permissions)
  • Data entered into the Velaro portal interface
  • Basic usage analytics to improve service quality

Velaro primarily uses Business Central's existing data and does not collect excessive personal data. No sensitive personal information such as home addresses or social insurance numbers is collected.

Use of Data

Any collected data is used solely for providing and improving the Velaro service:

  • To enable and manage portal access for customers and vendors
  • To process payments and manage portal services
  • To communicate necessary information to customers
  • To provide technical support and troubleshooting

Data is not used for unrelated purposes such as advertising. Aggregate usage data may be used internally to improve the product, with no personal identifiers included.

Third-Party Services

Velaro integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Payment Processors: Payment transactions are handled by secure payment processors, which receive billing information to process payments. Financial information such as credit card details is not stored by Velaro.
  • Microsoft Business Central: Velaro operates within your existing Business Central environment and accesses necessary data through standard Business Central APIs.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Velaro is committed to protecting your privacy rights under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California, USA). You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. Contact our support team to exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries.
